SUPER TROFEO WORLD FINAL

The Lamborghini World Final completes every Super Trofeo season since 2013. Every year, riders of all continental championships go to different tracks and take part in two 50-minute sprint races, where the overall winner is determined. like a Lamborghini world champion. The Lamborghini World Final is not only the biggest race weekend of the year, but also a real motorsport festival that attracts fans from all over the world.

The owners' parade, previews and entertainment in the paddock for fans are just some of the events organized as part of the event.

SUPER TROFEO TEAMS

All the cars that participate in the Lamborghini Super Trofeo championships are driven by private teams who use their expertise to fine tune them for drivers. For each Lamborghini Super Trofeo Continental Championship, there are ratings of drivers and teams.

The drivers and teams participating in the Continental Series compete against each other every year in the Lamborghini Grand Final, an event that ends the Super Trofeo sports season. Two qualifying sessions and two races determine the world series champions. Every year the Grand Final is held on different tracks, and in previous editions it has already affected the European, Asian and American continents.
